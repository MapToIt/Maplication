Our Mission:

To assist recruiters and attendees to find eachother painlessly.

How It Works:

Maplication is a online tool that assists job seekers and recruiters attending job fairs, hack-a-thons, or recruitment events, to connect and facilitate the hiring process. With this web application, job seekers will be able to see companies based on map location at an event to expedite their job search process. The interactivity allows job seekers to easily research information about companies before meeting up at the event. Features of the map would include hovering to see who is at that table and clicking to view company profiles. Additionally, the map will be searchable by company name and career field tags to highlight table locations.
